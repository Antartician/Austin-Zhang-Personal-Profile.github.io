Red Cross - Volunteer

The purpose of the American Red Cross (ARC) at Ithaca College is to coordinate student activites relating to programs sponsored by the American Red Cross, particularly the Tompkins County Chapter. These activities include, but are not limited to: blood drives, health and safety, emergency, homeless and youth services, as well as, any other projects agreed upon between the membership and the American Red Cross of Tompkins County. Activities such as these are to encourage Ithaca College students, staff, and faculty to participate in activities and events run for, and by the American Red Cross.


Asian American Alliance = Member

Ithaca College Asian American Alliance (AAA) is a student-led organization devoted to educating the Ithaca campus and community on Asian/Asian American cultures and issues. Every meeting, AAA looks through either a sociopolitical or cultural lens. Our approach produces a diversity of meetings that range from in-depth discussions on the Model Minority Myth to Karaoke Nights and DIY Dumpling-making. AAA is an outlet for education, recreation, social justice, and community. First and foremost, AAA strives to create a safe and engaging environment for all of its members.

Projects


  • IC Natural Lands Carbon Research
  • Ithaca Watershed Streamflow Research

IC Natural Lands Carbon Research

Collecting soil of IC wetlands to analyze for carbon content and percentage. The goal is to see if decaying organic matter in the wetlands is going into the soil or water. Soil is collected in bags and carried to the research lab to be studied. Ongoing research project.


Ithaca Watershed Streamflow Research

Analyzing stream data from USGS to create graphs using R. Many graphs are created for each month looking at factors of precipitation, temperatures, and much more. The goal is to see if the streamflow has changed over the years in Ithaca. Ongoing research project.
